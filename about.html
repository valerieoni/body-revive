A little about Body Revive

I am committed to bringing yoga's ancient knowledge into the contemporary world and using it to enhance and enrich people's lives. Everyone can benefit from yoga classes that focus on both physical and emotional wellbeing.

Body Revive offers 8 eight-week sessions of classes per year. Courses offered include Anusara-style Hatha Yoga plus workshops in related topics. The Yoga Center features well-trained, professional instructors and progressive teaching methods
